About

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Carlo Fischione, with a background in optimization, statistical signal processing, and communication theory. Prior to joining KTH, I received my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran, in 2018, advised by Arash Amini. I am currently a visiting researcher at Imperial College London, hosted by Deniz Gündüz at the Information Processing and Communications Lab. My current research focuses on machine learning over networks.

Research Interests

  • Optimization
  • Statistical Signal Processing
  • Theory of Machine Learning
  • Theory of Communication Systems
