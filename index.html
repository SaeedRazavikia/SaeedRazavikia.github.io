About

I am a last-year Ph.D. student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Carlo Fischione, conducting research at the intersection of optimization, statistical signal processing, and communication theory. Prior to joining KTH, I received my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran, in 2018, where I was advised by Arash Amini. My current research is supported by the WASP, focusing on machine learning over networks.

Research Interests

  • Optimization
  • Statistical Signal Processing
  • Theory of Machine Learning
  • Theory of Communication Systems
